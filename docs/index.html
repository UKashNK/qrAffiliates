Welcome to Earned Stars

Empowering businesses to earn from their everyday recommendations.

What We Do

We empower businesses to earn from the product recommendations they already make to their customers.

By using our platform, you can seamlessly recommend products related to your business that your customers may need or want, and get rewarded for these recommendations.

You already make these recommendations to your clients for free, the only difference is now you get paid.

How It Works

  1. Sign up on our platform

    It's very easy to get started.

  2. Choose the products you want to recommend

    We will set up a webpage with all these products for you that you can either add to your website or we can host it for you. Just like this one

  3. Share recommendations with your customers

    This could be as simple as placing a QR code at the counter with a caption like "My recommendations for smooth, silky hair."

  4. Earn rewards for every successful recommendation!

    When your customers visit your page and choose to purchase one of the products you recommend, you earn a commission on the sale.

Value for Your Business

Consider these examples:

At a Gym

A customer completes their workout and asks about the best activewear for their routine. The gym staff recommends a high-quality brand. If the customer purchases it, the gym has marketed the product for free.

At a Salon

A customer finishes a haircut and inquires about a shampoo or hair product to maintain their new style. The hairdresser suggests a trusted product from their inventory. If the customer buys it, the salon has marketed the product for free.

At a Cosmetics Shop

A customer asks about perfumes after a makeup session. You recommend a popular fragrance. If the customer decides to purchase, you have just marketed the product effortlessly for free.

With this platform, businesses like yours can turn these everyday recommendations into a new revenue stream.

  • You get paid for the recommendations you already make, and your customers get the products they need. It's a win-win for everyone involved.
  • You get to choose what products you recommend and how you recommend them.
  • This flexibility allows you to tailor your approach to your customers and operations.
